About WebDrop

WebDrop is a cutting-edge file sharing platform that prioritizes security, speed, and simplicity. Our service enables direct peer-to-peer file transfers without storing any data on servers, ensuring your files remain private and secure.

Security First

We utilize end-to-end encryption and direct peer-to-peer connections, meaning your files never touch our servers. This approach ensures maximum privacy and security for all file transfers.

Lightning Fast

By establishing direct connections between users, WebDrop enables rapid file transfers without the bottleneck of server uploads and downloads.

Simple to Use

No registration required. Simply open WebDrop, share your connection details, and start transferring files instantly. Our intuitive interface makes file sharing a breeze.

How to Use WebDrop

1

Open WebDrop

Visit WebDrop in your browser. No installation or signup required.

2

Connect with Peer

Share your connection ID or scan the QR code with another device.

3

Select Files

Drag and drop files into the upload area or click to select files.

4

Transfer Files

Files transfer directly to the connected peer with end-to-end encryption.

Pro Tips

  • Both devices must be connected to the internet
  • Keep the browser tab open during transfer
  • Use modern browsers for best performance
  • Large files may take longer to transfer

Troubleshooting

Connection Issues

Ensure both devices are online and using compatible browsers. Try refreshing the page or generating a new connection ID.

Transfer Failed

Check your internet connection and try reconnecting. Make sure the browser tab remains open during transfer.

QR Code Not Working

Ensure your camera has permission to scan QR codes and the code is clearly visible. Try manually entering the connection ID instead.
